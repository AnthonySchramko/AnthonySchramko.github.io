About

My name is Anthony Schramko, I am an med-science graduate transitioning to aspiring software engineer. I am based in Sydney, Australia and having provided technical support for the DoE I am loving learning the software-side of tech. Recently graduating with a Bachelors of Medical Science, I love anything medical (even the shows) and research.

If I'm not coding, I am most likely laughing at my cat doing random things, failing to organise boardgame nights with friends or watching MMA.
